Game Controls

[] : Move left

[] : Move right

[] : Jump

[ + ] : Jump left

[ + ] : Jump right

How to play

  • Jump on drums to reunite the guitar pick with its owner.
  • Use the LEFT and RIGHT keys to move or jump in that direction.
  • Charge the power of your jumps by holding DOWN and either LEFT or RIGHT.
